🛒 E-commerce Analytics Dashboard

Problem: E-commerce businesses need clear visibility into sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends to make data-driven decisions and improve revenue growth.

Approach: Analyzed e-commerce transactional data to identify key business patterns, customer purchasing behavior, revenue trends, and product performance. Created insights through data cleaning, exploration, and visualization.

Tools: Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL, Data Visualization, Excel

Business Objective: Understand sales performance, identify high-value customers and products, and discover opportunities for improving business strategy.

Key Insights:

  • Analyzed sales trends and revenue performance over time
  • Identified top-performing products and categories contributing to revenue
  • Explored customer purchasing behavior and segmentation patterns
  • Evaluated business KPIs to support data-driven decision making
  • Generated actionable recommendations for growth opportunities

Features:

  • Data cleaning and preprocessing workflow
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  • Sales and customer analytics
  • KPI tracking and business insights
  • Visual analysis of trends and performance metrics

🚴 Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

Description: This Tableau dashboard analyzes bike sales performance by focusing on revenue, profit, and customer demographics. It provides an interactive overview of how factors such as age groups, product categories, and geographic regions influence overall business performance.

Tools: Tableau, Excel

Business Objective: Evaluate sales performance and identify the most profitable products and customer segments driving revenue growth.

Key Insights:

  • Generated approximately $5.86M in profit with a 1.6% year-over-year increase
  • Adults (35–64 age group) are the primary contributors to sales and profit
  • Bike category generates the highest revenue compared to accessories and clothing
  • Products like water bottles and repair kits show strong individual performance
  • Sales are concentrated in North America and Europe

Features:

  • KPI cards for profit, revenue, and YoY growth
  • Product-level profit breakdown (bar chart)
  • Revenue trend over time
  • Customer segmentation (bubble chart)
  • Geographic sales distribution (map)
  • Interactive filters (year, gender, product category)
