🛒 AI-Powered E-commerce Customer Intelligence Platform

Problem: E-commerce businesses struggle to understand customer behavior, predict customer value, identify churn risks, and convert large volumes of customer and transaction data into actionable business insights.

Solution: Built an end-to-end analytics and AI platform that combines data engineering, machine learning, and Generative AI to analyze customer behavior and support data-driven decision making.

Tech Stack: SQL, dbt, Python (Pandas, scikit-learn), Streamlit, Machine Learning, LLM, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), Data Visualization

Key Features:

  • Designed SQL analytics models for customer, order, and revenue analysis
  • Built dbt data transformation pipelines for clean and reusable analytics datasets
  • Performed customer segmentation using behavioral and transactional features
  • Developed Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) analysis to identify high-value customers
  • Implemented churn prediction models to detect customers at risk of leaving
  • Created KPI dashboards tracking revenue, retention, customers, and product performance
  • Built an interactive Streamlit analytics application for business users
  • Integrated an AI Customer Intelligence Assistant using LLM + RAG to answer business questions from e-commerce data

Business Impact:

  • Improved understanding of customer purchasing behavior
  • Enabled personalized marketing strategies using customer intelligence
  • Supported retention campaigns through churn risk prediction
  • Provided natural-language access to analytics using Generative AI

🚴 Bike Sales Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

Description: This Tableau dashboard analyzes bike sales performance by focusing on revenue, profit, and customer demographics. It provides an interactive overview of how age groups, product categories, and geographic regions influence business performance.

Tools: Tableau, Excel

Business Objective: Evaluate sales performance and identify profitable products and customer segments driving revenue growth.

Key Insights:

  • Generated approximately $5.86M in profit with 1.6% YoY growth
  • Adults (35–64 age group) contribute the most sales and profit
  • Bike category produces the highest revenue
  • Water bottles and repair kits show strong product performance
  • Sales are concentrated in North America and Europe

Features:

  • Profit, revenue, and YoY KPI tracking
  • Product performance analysis
  • Revenue trend visualization
  • Customer segmentation dashboard
  • Geographic sales analysis
  • Interactive Tableau filters
