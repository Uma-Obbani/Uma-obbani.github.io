Customer Segmentation for Targeted Marketing

Problem: The business lacked a clear understanding of customer groups and their behavior, limiting the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Approach: Performed exploratory data analysis and applied K-Means clustering with feature engineering to segment customers based on transactional and behavioral patterns.

Tools: Python (Pandas, scikit-learn), SQL, Data Visualization

Impact: Identified distinct customer segments, enabling targeted marketing strategies, improved personalization, and more efficient budget allocation.
